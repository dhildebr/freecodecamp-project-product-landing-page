Tired of always losing your socks when you travel? Keep finding your journal crushed at the bottom of your bottomless backpack? Perhaps you've nicked yourself one time too many trying to dig around in your demiplane pockets. Never lose track of your gear again with Wizard Distentor's Partitioned Bag of Holding™.

Easy Organisation The partitioned bag comes with an easy to use organisational scheme right out of the box, and it's painless to sort your gear however you want.

Intuitive Design Always grab what you're after on the first try. The partitioned bag deciphers user intent to hand you whatever you seek without fuss or rummaging.

Safe Operation Weapons and tools are always removed handle first, and there's no danger of falling in – beings with complex souls like men and elves are prevented from going in deeper than the shoulder, but the entrance remains permeable to simpler creatures.

I'm an adventurer, which means the good people of Gielinor point me towards someone who deserves it and I leave a trail of corpses between here and there. To earn any money in this trade, you learn to strip everything you kill of anything sufficiently light or valuable, to sell when you get back to town. With the partitioned bag of holding, I can haul about the half dozen swords and axes I inexlicably found in a dragon's belly and not worry about losing that enchanted ring I found among the clutter.

I'm what you could call a power-user. Before the partitioned bag, everything went in one or a handful of sacks and pouches. My runestones tumbled among my scrolls, my scrolls went crashing into my potions, my potions spilled all over my journals – and a foul black slurry pooled beneath it all. Now everything is segregated into one of potentially numerous demiplanes. This sets a new standard for pocket dimension storage solutions!

When at last I return to civilisation, I'm always hauling a truly ludricrous number of fresh pelts into town. Before the partitioned bag of holding, I was limited by how high I could stack it all on my back, but now I'm limited only by my own strength. As a side benefit, I can stop getting the stink-eye from innkeeps for bringing a cloud of animal musk and a trail of blood in the door.
